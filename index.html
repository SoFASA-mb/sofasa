Welcome to SOFASA

SoFASA (SoFA), the School of Fine Arts Students Association (or Students of Fine Arts), is a School of Art student group. Our mandate as an organization is listed in our Constitution which can be found on our homepage under Links, or here. To apply to be a student representative on SoFA, all you need to do is follow the rules in the nomination form, also located on our homepage, or right here. Our members are commonly seen on campus and generally do not bite so please say/shout 'hello!' if you see us :)

STUDIO 460

This space has been transformed into an open studio - a space by students, for students. Come in and work on projects, take a break, meet new friends, critique together, share supplies, participate in workshops and so much more.
